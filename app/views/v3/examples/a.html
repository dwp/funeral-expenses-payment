How much you could get

Example A

The person who died was over 18 and lived in Northumberland. They had £150 in a bank account on the day they died. 

The person who's responsible for the funeral needed to give the bank a death certificate to release the money. They would be able to get a total payment of £1781.

Payment Amount
Cremation costs

This is the fee charged by Northumberland County Council.		 £920
Document costs

Death Certificate to release money from the bank account of the person who died.		 £11
Travel costs

The person responsible did not have any travel costs to arrange or attend the funeral.		 £0
Cost of moving the body

There were no costs to move the person who died over 50 miles to the funeral location.		 £0
Other funeral costs

This is the maximum amount you can get towards costs such as funeral director fees, a coffin or flowers. 		 £1000
Deductions

The person who died had £150 in a bank account which the person responsible for paying for the funeral has access to.		 -£150
Total £1,781
