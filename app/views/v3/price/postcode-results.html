The amount you may get for a:

  • burial is £1,079
  • burial and plot is £2,218
  • cremation is £850

This amount is for an adult funeral taking place after 6 April 2023. If the funeral was before 6 April 2023 the amount may be different.

If the person who died is under 18

Usually, it does not cost anything to bury or cremate a child under 18 or a baby stillborn after the 24th week of pregnancy. You can get more support for child funeral costs (opens in new tab).

Other costs you'll get help with

You’ll also get up to £1,000 for any other funeral expenses, such as funeral director’s fees, flowers or the coffin. There are other things that affect how much you’ll get.
