It currently takes 3 to 4 weeks to process a claim.

What we need

Before we can process your claim we will need a copy of:

  • the funeral bill or signed contract between you and the funeral director
  • any final statements from the accounts of the person who died

If you do not have a bill or signed contract, we will contact the funeral director for a copy after the funeral. If you can get a copy from your funeral director, or ask them to send it to us, we may be able to process your claim faster.

We will also need to contact you about any life insurance policies for the person who died.

How to send it

You can send us what we need by email or post.

You’ll need to include your full name, address and National Insurance number with anything you send, so we can match it to your claim.

Email

funeral.bills@dwp.gov.uk

Post

Freepost DWP, Funeral Payments

If you need to contact us

If you have new information about the funeral, call 0800 151 2012 and choose option 3.
