You can only get a Funeral Expenses Payment if the funeral was in the last 6 months

If the funeral was on 6 October 2023 it has been over 6 months. This means you cannot get a Funeral Expenses Payment.

Formal decision needed?

To get a formal decision you’ll need to complete a claim form and send it by post. You can get a claim form by going to GOV.UK and searching for ‘funeral costs’.
