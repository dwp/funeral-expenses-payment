You must get a qualifying benefit to get a Funeral Expenses Payment

If you’re not getting, or waiting to hear about, a qualifying benefit you cannot make a claim for Funeral Expenses Payment.

Need to apply for a qualifying benefit?

If you’re eligible for a qualifying benefit, you may be able to get a Funeral Expenses Payment. You’ll need to make your claim after you’ve applied for a qualifying benefit.

You can find out what benefits you may be entitled to by going to GOV.UK and searching for ‘benefits calculator’.
