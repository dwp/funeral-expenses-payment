You must be responsible for paying for the funeral to get a Funeral Expenses Payment

The person responsible for paying for the funeral may be able to get a Funeral Expenses Payment if they meet all other eligibility criteria.

Formal decision needed?

To get a formal decision you’ll need to complete a claim form and send it by post. You can get a claim form by going to GOV.UK and searching for ‘funeral costs’.
