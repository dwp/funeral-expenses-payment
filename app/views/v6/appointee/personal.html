If you're a personal appointee

We may ask you to provide proof you can act for the person who wants help with the funeral costs.

This could be a:

  • letter from the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) or Department for Communities (DfC) in Northern Ireland
  • court order appointing you as the claimant's Deputy or Guardian
  • lasting power of attorney (LPA) or power of attorney (PoA) in Scotland
