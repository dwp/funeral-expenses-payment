If you’re not getting, or waiting to hear about, a qualifying benefit you cannot make a claim for Funeral Expenses Payment.

If you’re eligible for a qualifying benefit

You’ll need to make your claim after you apply for a qualifying benefit and within 6 months of the funeral.

You can find out what benefits you may be entitled to by going to GOV.UK and searching for ‘benefits calculator’.

If a formal decision is needed

To get a formal decision you’ll need to complete a claim form and send it by post. You can get a claim form by going to GOV.UK and searching for ‘funeral costs’.
