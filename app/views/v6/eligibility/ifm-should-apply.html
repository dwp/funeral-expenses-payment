You may not be able to get a Funeral Expenses Payment

Usually it is only a surviving parent or adult child of the person who died that can get a Funeral Expenses Payment.

If all surviving parents and adult children cannot apply

You may be able to get a Funeral Expenses Payment if their relationship had broken down, or if all surviving parents and adult children are either:

  • in full-time education
  • living outside the UK
  • staying in hospital
  • living in a care home
  • in prison or remand
  • a fully-maintained member of a religious order
