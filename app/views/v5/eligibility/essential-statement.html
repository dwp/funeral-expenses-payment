Essential Statement

If the person who died left money in their estate the Funeral Expenses Payment made from Social Fund will have to be paid back from this.

The law states that funeral expenses must be paid before anything else is paid from the estate.

A funeral payment includes necessary burial or cremation fees, certain other specified expenses such as travel costs and up to £1000 for additional funeral expenses, such as the funeral director's fees, the coffin or flowers.

if the person who died had a pre-paid funeral plan or insurance policy, you will only get help for items not already covered by the plan or policy.
